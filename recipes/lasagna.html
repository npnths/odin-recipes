Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish beloved for its rich layers and comforting flavors. It typically consists of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with fillings such as ragù (a slow-cooked meat sauce), creamy béchamel, and sometimes ricotta or mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

Ragù

  • 500g beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 125ml red wine
  • 250ml broth
  • 1tbsp EVO oil
  • Salt & pepper

Assembly

  • Béchamel sauce
  • 250g lasagna sheets
  • 250g Parmigiano Reggiano
  • EVO oil

Steps

Ragù

  1. Heat 2tbsp EVO oil in a large pan, medium heat
  2. Add the chopped onion, carrot, celery and garlic. Cook for ~6 minutes
  3. Add the beef. Cook until browned breaking it with a spoon
  4. Pour the red wine and let it simmer for ~3 minutes
  5. Stir in the crushed canned tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, some salt and some pepper
  6. Reduce heat to the lowest and simmer until thickened (usually takes between 30 minutes and 1 hour)

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven (180°C or ~350°F)
  2. Spread a thin layer of ragù on the bottom of a baking dish
  3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets (slightly overlapping)
  4. Spread 1/3 of the ragù, 1/4 of the béchamel and sprinkle with Parmigiano
  5. Repeat for 3 layers (ading pasta within the layers)
  6. Add a final layer of pasta, the remaining béchamel and a generous sprinkle of Parmigiano
  7. Drizzle with EVO oil

Baking

  1. Cover the baking dish with tin foil and bake for 25 minutes
  2. Remove the foil and bake for another ~15 minutes, until golden and bubbly
  3. Let it rest for ~5 minutes before slicing and serving
